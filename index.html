Budget Ladakh Road Trip from Guntur

Pangong Lake (Pangong Tso) in Ladakh is a renowned high-altitude, saltwater lake at 4,350 meters, famous for its vivid, changing shades of blue, green, and red, framed by arid mountains. Stretching 160km between India and China, it remains a top tourist destination known for its intense, crystal-clear blue water, especially in summer.

Manali-Leh highway in 7 days. Stops: Keylong, Sarchu, Pangong. Under 25k with bike rental.

Weekend Getaways from Guntur

The Undavalli Caves, located about 22 km from Guntur (and 6 km from Vijayawada) on the banks of the Krishna River, are 4th-5th century monolithic rock-cut temples dedicated to Anantapadmanabha Swamy and Narisimhaswami. Carved out of sandstone, this four-story structure features a massive reclining Vishnu statue, intricate sculptures, and serves as a major protected monument of national importance in Andhra Pradesh.

Undavalli Caves (30min), Kondapalli Fort (2hrs), Nagarjuna Sagar (3hrs). Day trips with friends.
